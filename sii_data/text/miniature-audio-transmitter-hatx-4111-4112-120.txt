Miniature Audio Transmitter - HATX-4111/4112
Description
The HATX-4111/4112 is a miniature audio transmitter, which has an compact design. High spectral
purity and no sub harmonics provided by the SAW resonator oscillator type. The transmitter is
designed for room monitoring. With its built-in miniature electret microphone (Knowles) produces
excellent transmission of background noise.
The delivery is possible as with internal microphone (HATX-4111) or with external microphone (HATX4112). The transmitter has a transmission power of 10 mW.
Several scanners with fitted frequency range are recommended as suitable receiver
(see Model Group 110).
