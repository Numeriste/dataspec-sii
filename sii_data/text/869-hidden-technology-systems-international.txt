The MAG5 is an evolution of the Magnatik series and well known for its performance under harsh conditions.

The MAG5’s enclosure is made from machined acetal and
is very robust, water-resistant and tested to IP-67 level.

The MAG5 has a rechargeable battery which powers the unit
for at least 32 hours, and up to 16 days in standby mode.

Internal GPS and GSM-antennas:
The finely tuned GPS and GSM antenna are securely
concealed within the robust enclosure.

There are 6 different power modes available to reduce the unit’s
power consumption and take control of the operating time.
