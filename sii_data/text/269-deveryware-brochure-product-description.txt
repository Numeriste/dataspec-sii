Helping investigators
in the field to track targets

Additional application
of Deveryloc
to be downloaded (free)
on smartphones and tablets.

•Enables to track all mobile phones upon judicial
requisition and/or beacons from one or several
Deveryloc accounts.
•Aims at assisting field investigators to get in
real time their own locations, those of their
colleagues and those of the target(s)
so as to improve the coordination
of an ongoing action plan.
•Displays target-related events
that occurred (entry in an area,
movement, stopping ... ).
•Compatible with iOS iPhone
versions 4 and+, iPad 1 & 2,
Android* smartphone.
(*soon)
