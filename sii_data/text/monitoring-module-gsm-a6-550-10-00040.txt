Monitoring module - GSM-A6
General
The GSM-A6 is an especially designed GSM module for voice transmission. It is designed for
concealed installation in vehicles and is activated by an internal movement sensor.
In case of installation in vehicles, simply an appropriate positioning of GSM antenna and
microphone would be required. Other items are also suitable for installation (e.g. suitcase,
container).
Power supply occurs externally.
