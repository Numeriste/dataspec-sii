PV900 Cash Tracke r

Using ultra thin flexible technology the PV900 Cash
Tracker is disguised as a currency note . It can be
placed discreetly amongst cash notes, in a bag or
on a person. PV900 Cash Tracker will report its
position either on a timed basis, or when requested
from the control centre. The precise location is then
ca lcu lated from the cell information reported by the
PV900 module. Using highly integrated efficient
controllers Cash Tracker can report for up to 30
days on one charge of the batteries.
From the control centre each PV900 Cash Tracker
module can be individually located with the position
displayed on a map.
