Miniature Audio Transmitter - HATX-4104
Description
The HATX-4104 is a miniature transmitter with a PLL frequency processor. It is constructed using
SMD technology and is fitted with a highly sensitive miniature electret microphone. The transmitter is
enclosed in a metal housing. It is extremely compact and robust.
Several scanners with fitted frequency range are recommended as suitable receiver (see Model Group
110).
