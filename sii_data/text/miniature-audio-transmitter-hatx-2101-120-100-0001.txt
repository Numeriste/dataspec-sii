Miniature Audio Transmitter HATX-2101
Description
The HATX-2101 is a miniature printed circuit transmitter using low supply voltages from
1.5 to 4.5 V DC. It is constructed using SMD technology and fitted with a highly sensitive miniature
electret microphone. The transmitter can also be supplied with a remote microphone.
This miniature printed circuit transmitter is encapsulated in a special epoxy resin, is of extremely
rugged construction and suitable for concealed installation in a variety of items.
Several scanners with fitted frequency range are recommended as suitable receiver (see Model Group
110).
