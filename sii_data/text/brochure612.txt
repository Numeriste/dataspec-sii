Field/Site Services

capabilities ranging from system integration and installation, maintenance
support (on-call/on-site), product demonstrations, and operator and maintenance
training on our products. We are highly capable in responding to any technical
requirements needed by the customers. Our customers extend to both the military
and civilian sectors of various industries implementing L-3 Linkabit products.
