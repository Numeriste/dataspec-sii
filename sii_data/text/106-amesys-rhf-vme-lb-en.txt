Reception terminal for narrow multi
band demodulators

RHF VME WB is a HF wide band reception
unit in the 100 KHz to 30 MHz range
designed for listening and analysis of 2
MHz band width.
Designed with a VME structure, it offers a
wide range of output interfaces of digital
signal, complex with sampling rate of 2.4
mega pairs (I,Q).
