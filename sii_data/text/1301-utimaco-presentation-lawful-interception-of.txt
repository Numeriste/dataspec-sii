Summary and Recommendations
Lawful interception of LTE and VoLl-E is well standardized and
commonly supported by the network nodes
LI for RCS is not standardized yet, but can mostly be covered by
existing standards and tools
Implementation of LI in the networks can be complex and requires
Proper analysis of the detailed requirements
Tight integration with network elements of various vendors
Optional use of LI probes
High-capacity, high-performance mediation devices
Optional integration with other LI systems (e.g. 2G13G, fixedbroadband)
