Miniature Camera Transmitter - VCTX 5800/25C
Description
The VCTX 5800/25C is a small efficient video transmitter with an audio channel constructed with an
integral color-camera. It is intended especially for undercover use. This miniature transmitting device is
particularly useful in the areas of security and undercover surveillance.
Combined with the video receiver VRX 5800/32 (Model No: 130 100 0059) or VRX 5800/32 DX (Model
No: 130 100 0060), the VCTX 5800/25C forms an efficient video transmission system.
