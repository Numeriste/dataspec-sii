Verint Systems Ltd.
ENGAGE PI2
Passive, Tactical Off-Air Intelligence
ENGAGE™ PI2™ offers a passive off-air
interception solution for calls and SMSs over
GSM networks. In addition, ENGAGE PI2 can be
extended to support mobile satellite
communication networks, Thuraya and ISAT.
Secure and covert, ENGAGE PI2 passively
intercepts the cellular network where it operates
undetected by the network and targets. Acquiring
the signal off-air, ENGAGE PI2 breaks the encryption, produces and
monitors voice, SMS text messages, and GSM protocol. Further, ENGAGE
PI2 requires no connectivity to cellular providers.
Compact and powerful, ENGAGE PI2 offers advanced interception, flexible
deployment options and optimal portability in one viable solution.

ENGAGE GI2 - Tactical GSM and UMTS (3G) Monitoring
Robust Cellular Monitoring and Management in One Portable, Covert
Case
Acting as a GSM or UMTS (3G) base station, Verint®
ENGAGE™ GI2™ enables law enforcement agencies
and military forces to identify, intercept, track,
manipulate, and locate targets’ mobile phones during
everyday operations.
This suite of tactical solutions provides cellular
monitoring and management tools that offer the
following critical capabilities:


Remotely block and control target cellular communications



Intercept outgoing and incoming calls and SMS’s, including A5/1
encrypted networks



Locate cellular phones on GSM and UMTS (3G) networks

Flexible deployment options, including vehicles, fixed sites, and
aircrafts, and suitable for operations over an extended range
